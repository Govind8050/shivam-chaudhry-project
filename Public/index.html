Paper Plate Details

Paper plate :-Manufactured from 100% food-grade, biodegradable material. These plates feature a high-strength design with moisture-resistant coating, making them perfect for hot meals and liquids. Available in various sizes (6" to 12") with premium silver or printed leaf patterns.

Paper Glass Details

Paper Glass :- Our premium paper glasses come with a leak-proof polyethylene lining. They are designed for both hot and cold beverages like tea, coffee, and juices. Ranging from 100ml to 300ml, they offer a sturdy grip and are completely hygienic and easy to dispose of.

Juna Packing

Juna Packing (Scrubber) :- High-density stainless steel scrubbers designed for heavy-duty industrial and domestic cleaning. These are rust-proof, long-lasting, and engineered to remove tough stains without damaging surfaces. Available in professional bulk packing for wholesalers.

About Anastik Manufacturing Pvt Ltd

Anastik Manufacturing Pvt Ltd is a rapidly growing manufacturing company based in the Darbhanga district of Bihar. Built on the core values of quality, sustainability, and innovation, the company is committed to delivering reliable and eco-friendly utility products for both household and industrial use.

Under the visionary leadership of Shivam Kumar Chaudhary, Anastik Manufacturing Pvt Ltd has established itself as a trusted name in the regional manufacturing sector. Our leadership focuses on modern production practices, ethical business operations, and long-term customer satisfaction.

Our Mission
Our mission is to promote environmentally responsible manufacturing by replacing harmful plastic-based products with biodegradable and sustainable alternatives. We actively support the “Make in India” initiative by strengthening local manufacturing capabilities and generating employment opportunities in North Bihar.

What We Do
We specialize in the large-scale production of eco-friendly paper plates, paper glasses, and high-durability “Juna” steel scrubbers. Every product is manufactured using advanced machinery and undergoes strict quality control to ensure superior performance, hygiene, and durability.
